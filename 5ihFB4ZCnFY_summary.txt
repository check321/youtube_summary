Rosie, also known as Rose, shares her culinary favorites, including Kimi fried rice, a soju-beer mix called "so," and chongyang mayo sauce with dry squid. She introduces her new single "AP," inspired by a Korean drinking game, and recounts her journey from New Zealand to Australia, then to Korea, where she trained and debuted with Blackpink. Rosie discusses her love for spicy food and cilantro, her collaboration with Bruno Mars, and her fondness for Spam. She reflects on the emotional process of creating her solo album "Rosie," which captures her personal experiences over the past four years. Rosie dreams of retiring to own a cilantro farm and shares her excitement about her upcoming album release.
